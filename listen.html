The App

Finally we explain more in depth how our application LexEco operates.

Register

To get a more rewarding experience, you are welcome to become a member by creating a Lexeco account. Nevertheless, it is possible to use this app without a membership while still getting to use the scan function, which is the core of LexEco. Register your card to the application through this page. This will directly be linked to every purchase at any store, and you will be able to get the data collection for every previous purchase. Your privacy will never be at stake when using this app, as we follow international regulations when it comes to privacy.

Scan Page

Hover the barcode scanner over a product and touch the button to register the scan. You will then be notified whether the scanned product was made by sustainable standards.

Menu

The menu is created to sort all of the tools in the app in one place. In the menu you can find the search function, giving you the opportunity to search for any products or companies you might want to buy from. The menu also gives you access to other functions of the app, such as rewards and scan history, which will be explained further.

Rewards

The reward page contains the points earned every time you have bought sustainably. You will be given a discount based on the purchase of the products and the companies behind the production. The points will vary dependent on the sum of the purchase. To get a discount, you must be able to reach a score.

Scans

All of your previously scanned products will easily be accessible in the scan history page and sorted by “sustainable” or “unsustainable”. By clicking on the product on the history list, you will be given more information about the product.
