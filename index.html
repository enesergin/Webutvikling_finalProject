Why is the problem relevant?

There is no reason to make a service for the sake of it. It is of great importance to create something that is needed or would make difference in society. As a team the 12th. sustainable goal was something we wanted to fight towards. While brainstorming we realised that we ourselves had struggles with choosing ecological products. Therefor, we created a survey to get an idea of how we could help the people of Norway choose sustainable and whether that was something they needed.

When asked if it was of importance whether a product is made in a sustainable way 30% of the results indicated "Yes" and 45% "Somewhat", leaving no at 25%.

Another crucial point we had to find out, was whether people find it difficult to recognise sustaiable products when shopping. Only 10% answered no, meaing a breathtakingly 90% find it challenging.

The next question asked whether the unit would be more inclined to buy a product if they knew it was sustainable. The results were luckily in the futures favor as 74% answered "Yes".

Lastly, we asked if the units would use a simple tool that could help them while shopping sustainably, yet again the respons was positive. 36% answered "Yes", 46% answered "Yes - if it is free and simple", leaving "No" at 18%.
