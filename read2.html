"A simple solution to a complex problem"

The Challenge

Humanity is facing one of its greatest challenges right now – the climate crisis. The climate changes, such as global warming, are primarily due to human activities.

The arising issue is “How to make it easier to act sustainably?”. From a social viewpoint, it can be difficult to keep track of sustainable and unsustainable products. This results in being more challenging to act sustainably.

From a technological perspective, the challenge is to sort the given information. The application makes it easier to collect necessary information in one place.

Since the human activity has had a huge negative impact on the environment, we can not disregard the great potential we as humans have, to stop the climate changes.

Our Solution

By sustainable production, we are able to reduce the environmental damage due to gas emissions and irresponsible consumption.

Our solution is presented to you with our LexEco application. The application is universally designed with the ease of usability in mind.

By downloading our application and joining the LexEco family, you will ensure that the products you consume are all sustainable.

Lexeco is a free barcode-scanner for your device. By using your device´s camera, the barcode-scanner will automatically recognize if the product is sustainable or not, applying to both the materials and the production. If the product you want to buy is unsustainable, sustainable options will appear on the screen.

Our solution is based on independent work with an initiative to help both the environment and society.

Register

To get a more rewarding experience, you are welcome to become a member by creating a Lexeco account. Nevertheless, it is possible to use this app without a membership while still getting to use the core of LexEco, the scan function. Register your card to the application through this page. This will directly be linked to every purchase at any store, and you will get the data for every previous purchase. Your privacy will never be at stake when using this app.

Scan Page

Hover the barcode scanner over a product and touch the button to register the scan. You will then be notified whether the scanned product was made by sustainable standards.

Menu

The menu is created to sort all of the tools in the app in one place. In the menu you can find the search function, giving you the opportunity to search for any products or companies you might want to buy from. The menu also gives you access to other functions of the app, such as rewards and scan history, which will be explained further.

Rewards

The reward page contains the points earned every time you have bought sustainably. You will be given a discount based on the purchase of the products and the companies behind the production. The points will vary dependent on the sum of the purchase. To get a discount, you must be able to reach a score.

Scans

All of your previously scanned products will easily be accessible in the scan history page and sorted by “sustainable” or “unsustainable”. By clicking on the product on the history list, you will be given more information about the product.
