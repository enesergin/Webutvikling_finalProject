Humanity is facing one of its greatest challenges right now – the climate crisis. The climate changes, such as global warming, are primarily due to human activities.

The arising issue is “How to make it easier to act sustainably?”. From a social viewpoint, it can be difficult to keep track of sustainable and unsustainable products. This results in being more challenging to act sustainably.

From a technological perspective, the challenge is to sort the given information. The application makes it easier to collect necessary information in one place.

Since the human activity has had a huge negative impact on the environment, we can not disregard the great potential we as humans have, to stop the climate changes.

12 | Responsible Consumption and Production

We were motivated by the 12th UN goal, and want to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns

Our target group is primarily those who experience challenges while acting environmentally friendly. Bigger communities consist of all types of people. Some people have sufficient knowledge about the issue, and others have barely heard about the issue. We want to help as many people as achievable through this product and service and have designed the application to be as easy as possible.
By sustainable production, we are able to reduce the environmental damage due to gas emissions and irresponsible consumption.
To move forward our main focus need to be on the solution. By now, most people have heard about the climate crisis at least once. Constantly, it is presented to us that it is important to act in a sustainable matter, but we are not told enough how to do so.
Do you want to act sustainably, but don’t know where to start? You have arrived to the tool that is going to save both, your time and energy! Our solution is presented to you as the Lexeco application. The application software is universally designed with the ease of usability in mind.
By downloading our application and joining the Lexeco membership, your store visits will become a lot easier.
Lexeco is a free barcode-scanner for your device. By using your device´s camera, the barcode-scanner will automatically recognize if the product is sustainable or not, applying to both the materials and the production. Sustainable options will appear on the screen, if the product you want to buy is unsustainable.
As a member, you collect points every time you a sustainable product, and after a number of specific points you will get discount on your favorite products.
Our solution is based on independent work with an initiative to help both the environment and the people in the society. The evidence of our creative and relevant work is in our solution.
How does the app work?
To get a better, more rewarding experience, you are welcome to join our membership club by creating a Lexeco account. You can also attach your credit card or bank account to the application through this page. This will directly be linked to every purchase at any store, and you will be able to get the data collection for every previous purchase.
Next the barcode scanner automatically detects and scans the code without having to press any buttons. The flashlight support tool will be helpful in environments of low light. The question icon is another support tool if you face any problems during the scan. You will get a list over frequently asked questions and the contact information of the customer support. The search tool will help you search for specific products. Lastly, the scanner has its own history button showing previous scans.
The menu is created to sort all of the tools in the app, in one place. Firstly, we have the login-page. Here you can connect the application to your personal preferences and shopping. Next, we have a search bar, giving you the opportunity to search for any products or companies you might want to buy from. The next page in the application is the reward-page, registering your points gained throughout the shopping. The menu also has an option for viewing the scan history. The settings page consists of preferences, receipts, notifications, subscription to the calendar, privacy policy, terms of use and data collection. You also have the opportunity to change the application language to your own preference. The support tool is designed to answer the customers questions and help out with any problems, both technical and non-technical problems.
The reward page contains the points earned every time you have bought sustainably. You will be given a discount based on the purchase of the products and the companies behind the production. The points will vary dependent on the sum of the purchase. To get a discount, you must be able to reach a score. Different companies will have different point requirements.
All of the scanned products will be included in the history and sorted by “sustainable” or “unsustainable”. By clicking on the product on the history list, you will be able to get the product information including the date of the scan. You will also be able to add the total of the product.
To create this product and service, we are collaborating with companies we trust based on research and meetings. It is important for us to ensure that our partners have the same vision as us and also operate in a sustainable way. The challenge occurs when the production happens abroad where it could be different working requirements and conditions. Since safety and environment primarily is our focus, we are making sure that the regulations are in accordance to the Norwegian law. Nevertheless, our partners are mostly local. If they are abroad, they are certified in schemes with strict rules.
